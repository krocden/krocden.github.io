About Me

I’m a software developer based in Montreal, Quebec. Since I was young, I’ve been fascinated by games. I’ve always wanted to design and make my own game which led to me studying in Computer Science. I’ve been programming since I was 17 and it’s been an amazing experience so far being able to use my imagination and see my work happen in real time. I enjoy developing new skills and I’m passionate about my work. I love interacting with people and working as a team. I’ve previously completed a Computer Science degree (DEC) in Champlain College and I recently acquired my Bachelors of Computer Science from Concordia University.
Proficient in Java, C#, C++ and many more.
